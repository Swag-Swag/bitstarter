Swagfone OS is the revolutionary new mobile phone operating system that will harnesses the full capabilites of upcoming smartphones. Based on a linux variant, this OS is designed to provide the user with the responsiveness and the cutting egde power required for handing apps, music, videos and web surfing. Full customization allows the user to truely make their phone theirs and look and feel the way they want it. Swagfone OS is also a developer's dream, with the dev package making it even easier to deploy your own apps in SwagShop(the app marketplace). Swagfone is secure, with a built in SwagDefense system that blocks and alerts the user of anything suspicious ensuring the user has peace of mind. Swagfone will soon be compatible with the leading brands such as Samsung, Apple and many other mobile makers.

Swagfone OS is the creation of a startup company called Swag Industries based in New Zealand. It is comprised of Univeristy Students who were passionate about mobile technology but were left dissapointed with current Smartphone software. Swag Industries decided to try make their own OS and enter the market, confident that their product will meet the needs of the user. Smartphones are getting closer to current computers in terms of memory and processing power but the current OS on offer hardly make the most of the hardware. Swagfone OS seamlessly bridges this gap and makes your phone truely "smart". Experience more from your Smartphone with Swagfone OS. Discover the next generation of Smartphone software with Swagfone OS.
